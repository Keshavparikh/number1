Compile your evidence, and send in one click

Airport operations professionals can spend dozens of hours each month manually mailing communications to pilots. With Pattern, simply assemble your evidence in the dashboard, write your note, and click send. We’ll mail the letter same-day, and let you know when the recipient has viewed the evidence online.
